Year: 2016 –– 2017
Award: CES Innovations Awards 2017
I worked on GuardPeanut from square one with a small team of Designers and Engineers. The goal was to create a Smart Motion Alarm to protect everyday objects (e.g. bag, bike, laptop).

I did various tasks from ideation to production. I worked on features, user interfaces and packaging. I also did some frontend engineering, product management and QA.

GuardPeanut was released in November 2016. The product is available worldwide for $29.
